PayPal
PayPal has an app that when linked to a bank account can be very easy to send money through.

Through the app or online, once signed in to an account people can hit 'send' and search for the following email address: 1ccsplym@gmail.com. This will bring up the First Church of Christ Scientist account and then the amount can be chosen to contribute.

Or someone can go directly to the website: www.paypal.me/1ccsplym to donate.


Zelle
Zelle is the service provider which Chase and many other banks use for "Quick Pay".

Zelle has an app or anyone can go to www.zellepay.com/ to set up an account and transfer money to the church.

For banks that already use Zelle (including Chase), you will be taken directly to the Bank's website to complete the transaction.

All contributions should be sent to 1st Church of Christ Scientist (note it must be spelled as 1st not First to send to the right account). 

If anyone is unsure if they are sending to the right account, they can send $.01 and I can confirm once payment is received.
