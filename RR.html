Christian Science Reading Room

Plymouth, Michigan

All are WELCOME!

Christian Science Reading Rooms are public spaces where anyone can learn about  spiritual healing either through studying in the Reading Room or by signing out materials  on loan. 

Available for public use are:

                    Bible Resources ( dictionaries, commentaries,etc)
                    Reference Library (many Bible translations)
                    Computer for research
                    Quiet places to read,study and reflect

We also welcome all to events like web talks, radio programs, and reading groups that meet regularly.

Published materials about Christian Science are available to read there, borrow, or purchase, including the core teachings of Mary Baker Eddy. Our bestseller is "Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures" by Mary Baker Eddy.

Advancing spiritual healing since 1888, we're a local resource for the Plymouth community and greater Detroit, and we're here to serve you.

We're affiliated with First Church of Christ Scientist, Plymouth, in the same building.
